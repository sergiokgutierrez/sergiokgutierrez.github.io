Meet the Liasons

Gabrielle Sulkowski

gsulkows@gmu.edu

Gabrielle is a second year graduate student at Mason, and is pursuing a Master's in Educational Psychology. Prior to graduate study, she received an A.S. from NVCC and a B.S. from Mason. While at NVCC, she served as VP for Phi Theta Kappa International Society. During her undergraduate study at Mason, she served as VP for Mason IFEPM and Historian for Psi Chi and is an Outstanding Senior Award recipient. Outside of university life, she is a substitute teacher and private piano instructor. She enjoys spending time with her family, engaging in photography, playing the piano, painting, singing, and long-distance running in her free time. After completing her Master's, Gabrielle hopes to become a professor and looks forward to pursuing a PhD.

Alyssandra Malinish A.S.

amalinis@masonlive.gmu.edu

Alyssandra is a senior at Mason, and majors in Community Health with a concentration in Clinical Science. She possesses a unique perspective as she's transferred from NVCC to both JMU and GMU. As a student at NVCC, she served as VP for Phi Theta Kappa International Honor Society. At Mason, she's participated in the Arlington Fellows Global Health Fellows Program, worked as a bioethics intern, and now works as a bioethics research assistant and serves as President of Mason IFEPM. Outside of university life, she works as an emergency department scribe and is a certified EMT. She enjoys traveling in her spare time, and has participated in several international humanitarian missions. After graduation, Alyssandra looks forward to pursuing an MPH and then a PhD.
